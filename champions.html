About Champions Sports Bar

Champions Sports Bar in Biddeford, Maine, is a vibrant nightspot where sports enthusiasts and music lovers come together. Enjoy live music, karaoke, and various sports matches in a lively atmosphere that promises unforgettable nights.

Venue Details

  • Type: Sports Bar / Live Music Venue
  • Location: 15 Thornton St. Biddeford, ME
  • Hosts: Live music performances
  • Atmosphere: A lively, welcoming local hangout with a tight-knit crowd, big game energy, and regular live music and karaoke. Champions blends classic sports bar vibes with a fun, community-driven atmosphere where both fans and musicians feel at home.
