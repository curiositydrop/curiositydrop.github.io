Support Maine Music

BANDtroductions is built to connect bands, musicians, fans, venues and local businesses across Maine. Our sponsors help keep the platform growing while putting their brand in front of an active local music community.

Why Sponsor BANDtroductions?

Sponsoring BANDtroductions gives your business visibility in front of local bands, working musicians, live music fans and venues throughout Maine. Whether you're a music store, tattoo studio, brewery, restaurant, print shop, rehearsal space, photographer, recording studio or local service business, sponsorship gives you a chance to be seen where Maine's music community connects.

Sponsors may be featured on this page, highlighted across select pages on the site, and placed in premium featured locations depending on sponsorship level.
