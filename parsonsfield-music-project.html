About Parsonsfield Music Project

Parsonsfield Music Project is a group of seasoned musicians from Southern Maine bringing together the songs they grew up on with a passion for live performance and jamming.

Covering artists like Tom Petty, CCR, The Beatles, ZZ Top, and blending in summer country, 90s country, and bluesy rock, PMP delivers a fun, familiar, and energetic live experience.

Built on experience and a shared love of music, they’re all about playing what they love and connecting with audiences wherever they go.

Band Details

  • Genre: Classic Rock, Rock Blues, 90s Country
  • Looking For: New venues
