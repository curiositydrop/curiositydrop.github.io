About BANDtroductions

BANDtroductions was created to make it easier for musicians, bands, and venues to connect within the local music scene.

Finding the right band members, discovering new artists, or booking live music shouldn’t be complicated. BANDtroductions exists to bring those pieces together in one place.

For Bands

Bands can showcase who they are, what they play, and whether they’re looking for new members. Instead of posting across multiple groups and hoping the right person sees it, bands can be discovered by musicians actively searching for a project.

For Musicians

If you’re a singer, guitarist, drummer, bassist, or any kind of musician looking for a band, BANDtroductions helps you get noticed. Submit your profile, share a performance video, and let bands find you.

For Venues

Local venues are the backbone of live music. BANDtroductions highlights bars, restaurants, and stages that support musicians and gives bands a place to discover new venues looking for talent.

For the Local Scene

Music communities grow when artists, venues, and fans can find each other easily. BANDtroductions is built to support that connection and help the local scene continue to grow.

Submit Your Profile

If you're a band looking for members, a musician searching for a band, or a venue that supports live music, you can submit your information to be featured on the site.

All submissions are reviewed before appearing on the platform.

Thanks for being part of the scene.
