About Matt Roller

Matt Roller is a greater Portland, Maine–based independent musician and multi-instrumentalist known for his raw, self-produced sound and emotionally direct songwriting.

Blending elements of punk, alternative rock, and DIY production, his music pairs gritty energy with introspective lyricism rooted in themes of addiction, recovery, and the pressures of modern life.

Operating as a one-man project, Matt handles every aspect of his releases, embracing authenticity over polish while carving out a voice that is both confrontational and reflective within the underground music scene.
