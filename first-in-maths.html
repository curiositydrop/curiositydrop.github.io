About First in Maths

First in Maths, Portland, Maine’s indie rock gem, delivers punchy riffs, dancey beats, and memorable hooks. While the UK-inspired “Maths” nods to influences from Britpop and garage rock, their catalog balances 90’s alt-indie nostalgia, 2000’s dance rock sweat, and a trying-not-to-jamband-but-damn-we-can-jam energy. Captivating block parties, house parties, and Portland bars, these four guys singing about Cherry Coke wear their hearts on their flannel sleeves.
