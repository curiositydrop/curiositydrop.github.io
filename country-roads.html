About Country Roads

Country Roads is a highly professional, family friendly southern Maine modern country band that has been playing throughout Maine and New Hampshire for over 11 years. We have a well established Facebook page that features nearly 5,000 followers. We have a large array of performance videos available to view on our Facebook page.
