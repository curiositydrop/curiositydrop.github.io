Learn more about Burning Time

Burning Time has been playing together since 2014 with a sound that has been described as heavy meets melodic rock. They have played in numerous venues with a wide variety of artists and have developed a loyal fan base. Whether performing for hundreds at local clubs or thousands at festivals, BT delivers an unforgettable performance and an experience that won't soon be forgotten! Their mission? Bring a unique sound to the world and to keep the spirit of rock alive!
