About Inside Wants Out

Inside Wants Out is a guitar-centric melodic rock and blues group that joined forces in 2022 out of years of playing across the area. With a combination of influences spanning decades, the band blends sounds inspired by artists like Counting Crows, The Allman Brothers, and the MTV-era hits many listeners grew up on.
