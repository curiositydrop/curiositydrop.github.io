About Project Firebird

Project Firebird is a six piece cover band whose roots are in classic rock, but with a growing number of other decades and genres by popular request. We’re based in Portland with travel across Maine and state lines! Public & Private bookings available. We work with specialty and costume themes as well, especially 80’s nights. Let’s Party!

Band Members

  • Steve Saucier bass/backup vocals
  • Ryan Brown vocals
  • JT Leonard, guitar/backup vocals
  • Jay Ferrell electric/acoustic/backup vocals
  • Logan Cutlip drummer
