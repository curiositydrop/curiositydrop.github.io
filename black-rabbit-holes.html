About Black Rabbit Holes

BRH is the highly self-indulgent recording project of Eric Hartmann (hi there) and a bunch of rotating adventurous friends. I'm always looking for reckless friends to collaborate on recording. Have you ever asked, "what if Fall Out Boy's favorite band was Tool?" Probably not, but listen to the single and have a good laugh. And if you like it and want to throw down... get in touch.
