About SquirrelNutz

SquirrelNutz is a Maine-based drummer out of Bar Harbor with a lifetime of playing experience across a wide range of styles.

Having performed with classic rock, country, oldies, variety, rock, and Top 40 bands, SquirrelNutz brings versatility, steady rhythm, and a love for playing live music.

Not focused on flashy, over-the-top drumming, the goal is simple: keep the groove locked in, support the music, and help the band sound solid.
